# PRD: <feature name>

## Problem

What's broken or missing today, in concrete terms. No marketing voice.

## Users

Who feels the problem. Which roles or user types. One paragraph each.

## Success metrics

How we will know it worked. Measurable. At least one quantitative and one qualitative metric.

## Scope

Bulleted list of what this feature does.

## Non-scope

Bulleted list of what this feature explicitly does NOT do. Spend equal effort on this — it protects against scope creep.

## User stories

3–8 stories of the form: "As a <user>, I can <action>, so that <outcome>."

## Risks

Things that could make this fail. Each with a one-line mitigation.

## Open questions

Anything still ambiguous. Each tagged with who needs to answer.

## Strategic briefing diff

What was updated in `docs/products/<slug>.md` (one paragraph, or "no changes needed").
